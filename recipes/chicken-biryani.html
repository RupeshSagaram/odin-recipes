Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic one-pot dish that combines marinated chicken with fragrant basmati rice and a blend of spices. Here's a traditional Chicken Biryani recipe:

Ingredients

For the marination:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)

For the rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4-5 cups water for boiling
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1-2 cinnamon sticks
  • Salt to taste

For the biryani:

  • 3 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 cup fried onions
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk

Steps to cook biryani

Marinating the chicken:
  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and chopped coriander-mint leaves.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight for the best flavor.
Cooking the Rice
  1. Bring water to boil in a large pot. Add soaked basmati rice, bay leaves, cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon sticks, and salt.
  2. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% done. Drain the water and set aside.
Preparing Fried Onion:
  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Fry thinly sliced onions until golden brown and crispy. Remove and set aside.
Assembling and cooking the biryani:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a heavy-bottomed, oven-safe pot, add a layer of marinated chicken.
  3. Top the chicken with a layer of partially cooked rice.
  4. Continue layering, finishing with a layer of rice on top.
  5. Drizzle saffron-infused milk over the rice for color and aroma.
  6. Sprinkle fried onions over the rice.
  7. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or aluminum foil. Seal the edges to trap the steam.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the rice is fully cooked.
  9. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork once it's cooked.
  10. Serve hot, garnished with additional fried onions and chopped coriander-mint leaves.

Enjoy your delicious and aromatic chicken biryani with raita or side salad!
