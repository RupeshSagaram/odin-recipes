Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a delicious and popular North Indian dish that features cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. This dish is known for its perfect balance of spices and creamy texture, making it a favorite among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) - 250g
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Onion - 1 large
  • Tomatoes - 3 medium-sized
  • Cashew nuts - 10-12 pieces
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) - 1 teaspoon
  • Fresh cream - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt
  • Sugar - 1/2 teaspoon (optional, to balance the acidity of tomatoes)
  • Coriander leaves - for garnish

Instructions for cooking

  1. Cut the paneer into bite-sized cubes and set aside.
  2. Boil water and blanch the tomatoes for a few minutes. Peel off the skin, puree the tomatoes, and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and 1 tablespoon of butter. Add finely chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the tomato puree to the pan. Cook until the mixture thickens, and the oil begins to separate from the tomatoes.
  6. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
  7. Add the cashew nut paste to the pan. Stir continuously to avoid lumps and cook until the mixture thickens.
  8. Gently add the paneer cubes to the gravy. Pour in the fresh cream, and mix well. Simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala over the dish and give it a final stir. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.

Your Paneer Butter Masala is ready to be served! Enjoy it hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
